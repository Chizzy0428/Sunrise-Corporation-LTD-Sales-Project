# Profit and Sales Dashboard – Sunrise Cooperation Ltd (2014–2017)

## Overview

This project showcases an interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze the sales and profitability performance of *Sunrise Cooperation Ltd* from **2014 to 2017**. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into clear, actionable insights to support business and executive decision-making.

The repository includes both the **dashboard image** and the **Excel dataset** used for the analysis.


## Objectives

The dashboard was designed to:

* Monitor **total sales, profit, and customer count**
* Identify the **best and worst performing years**
* Analyze sales by **region, state, product category, and sub-category**
* Highlight **top customers by profit contribution**
* Detect **loss-making states** and underperforming regions
* Understand customer distribution by **age group**



## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI Desktop
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Excel (data source)



## Key Dashboard Features

* **KPI Cards:** Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Customers, Best/Worst Year, and Number of States
* **Trend Analysis:** Year-over-year sales and profit performance
* **Customer Analysis:** Top 10 customers by profit
* **Regional & Product Analysis:** Sales by region, category, and sub-category
* **Geographic Performance:** Bottom 5 states by profit
* **Interactivity:** Year and region slicers for dynamic analysis



## Key Insights

* **2017** was the strongest year in both sales and profit.
* The **Technology** category consistently outperformed other categories.
* A small group of customers generated a large share of total profit.
* Some states recorded losses, indicating areas needing cost or strategy review.
* The **West region** led in sales performance, while the Central region underperformed.
